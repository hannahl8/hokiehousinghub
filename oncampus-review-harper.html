5.0

Harper Hall was nearby West End and D2, so I never had to walk far for a meal. It was also very close to McComas gym, so I made it to some of the early 8am yoga classes on time. The only negatives are that it was pretty far from all my classes. I had to walk to McBryde and new classroom building a lot. I honestly liked it though; I got lots of exercise and I never gained the freshman 15!!! :) Harper Hall is also suite style dorm so 6 girls shared a common room and 2 bathrooms, 2 showers, and 3 bedrooms (2 girls each bedroom). It rocked and I would live there again if I could!
