5.0

Hunters Ridge was so awesome. It is walking distance to food lion, so grocery shopping was barely even a chore. The bus stop is right in front of the apartment building, and the ride to campus was so short. There is a walking path right outside that was 2 miles long, with gorgeous views. My roommate had a dog so I would take her on walks on this path every morning, it was an awesome routine. I genuinely have no complaints about this place, it was incredible, and I highly recommend it to anyone looking for a place to live! :)
