Hannah Lyons

3.5

I lived in a 5-bedroom, 2 full bath, 2 half bath, 2 full kitchen apartment. I paid extra for the master bedroom which had the half bath and walk in closet all to myself. I absolutely loved it! I am a bit of a germaphobe so having a bathroom to myself rocked. Since there were 2 kitchens it made sharing space between 5 girls easy. If there was only one kitchen, we definitely would have struggled with fridge space. The balcony rocked I loved sitting out on it. Foxridge was also really beautiful, I would take walks and I could walk miles in just the neighborhood because it was HUGE. All the farms around were nice, except after rain it would definitely stink like cow poop. The only reason I give Foxridge a 3.5/5 is because it was so far from everything on campus. I didn't have a car so the bus ride to campus or downtown was long. If that's not important to you then I definitely recommend living at Foxridge!
