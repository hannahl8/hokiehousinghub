I'm living with the same roommates as freshman year so that's great. I have the master and its super big with its own bathroom, but my roomates have very small rooms and share the common space bathroom which isn't as nice, and disproportionately priced because i only pay $15 more a month for my own bathroom and huge room. I love the kitchen, its spacious with a lot of storage. Every time we have a maintenance request its completed the same day. The living room feels big for the small furniture, but at least it came furnished. The price is a little high at $850 and I've never used the amenities which include a small gym, pool, and hot tub at the leasing office. The bus routes are amazing and super close, I can walk out to it a minute before the bus gets there.

Terrace View presents itself as a convenient off-campus housing option, but beneath its facade lies a disheartening reality of neglect, mismanagement, and utter disappointment. From deplorable living conditions to lackluster amenities and apathetic management, Terrace View is a living nightmare for its unfortunate residents. Let's start with the state of the apartments themselves. Upon moving in, residents are met with a slew of issues ranging from pest infestations and mold growth to malfunctioning appliances and plumbing problems. The units are poorly maintained, with outdated fixtures, dingy carpets, and a pervasive odor that permeates every corner. Attempts to address these issues with management are met with indifference or delayed responses, leaving residents to suffer in substandard living conditions. The amenities promised by Terrace View are nothing short of a cruel joke. The fitness center resembles a dilapidated warehouse with broken equipment and a complete lack of cleanliness. The pool area is a breeding ground for bacteria and algae, rendering it unusable for residents' seeking relaxation or recreation. Common areas are often littered with trash and debris, reflecting the overall neglect and disregard for residents' well-being. Location-wise, Terrace View fails to provide the convenience and accessibility it claims to offer. Situated in a less-than-desirable neighborhood plagued by crime and noise pollution, residents feel unsafe and isolated in their surroundings. The lack of nearby amenities and entertainment options further exacerbates feelings of frustration and disillusionment, leaving residents longing for a sense of community and connection. But perhaps the most egregious aspect of Terrace View is its management's blatant disregard for residents' concerns and grievances. Requests for maintenance and repairs are routinely ignored or dismissed, leaving residents to fend for themselves in the face of ongoing issues. Attempts to communicate with management are met with unresponsiveness or excuses, leaving residents feeling powerless and unheard in their own homes. In summary, Terrace View Apartments is a cesspool of neglect and incompetence that should be avoided at all costs. From its deplorable living conditions to its lackluster amenities and apathetic management, Terrace View fails to provide the safe, comfortable, and welcoming living environment that residents deserve.
